For my project on day and night in p5, I initially created an algorithm that used conditionals to change the visual elements of the program. I used a ball to represent the sun and moon, and changed its position and color based on whether it was day or night. Additionally, I made the background change progressively to simulate the transition from day to night. However, I found that the transitions between day and night were not as smooth as I wanted them to be. So, I decided to try a different approach and use transitions instead of conditionals.
