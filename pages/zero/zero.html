no. Zero

My first project using p5 was an exciting adventure. At first, I didn't know where to begin, but I quickly realized that the possibilities were endless. I experimented with different shapes, colors, and effects, trying to create something unique and interesting. At first, I started with simple shapes and colors, trying to get a feel for how p5 works. Then, I started adding more complexity, experimenting with gradients, shadows, and other effects. I was surprised at how easy it was to create stunning visual effects using p5. One of the things I enjoyed most about the project was the sense of freedom it gave me. I could try out different ideas, and if something didn't work, I could easily undo it and start over. This made the creative process fun and enjoyable, and it encouraged me to keep trying new things. As I worked on the project, I discovered new features and techniques that I hadn't even thought of before. This allowed me to take my project in new and exciting directions, and it helped me to push the limits of what I thought was possible. In the end, I was really proud of the project that I had created. It was a true reflection of my creativity and my ability to use p5 to bring my ideas to life. I can't wait to see what other projects I can create using this powerful tool, and I look forward to exploring new techniques and ideas as I continue to develop my skills.
